Description

Beautiful, crispy, non-fungible fries guaranteed to energize you for the next NFT drop. Perfectly fired and salted to fend off saltyness of paying high gas fees.

Ingredients

  • Potatoes
  • Salt
  • Questionable oil of choice

Steps

  1. Thinly cut potatoes
  2. place cut potatoes in dryer of questionable origin for 3 minutes
  3. Pull out of fryer and salt instantly
  4. Mix up fries to distribute salt
  5. Plate fries and serve
