Shake

Description

Sweet chocolate shake. Not your ordinary fungible shake. This one of a kind non-fungible chocolate shake is said to have been blessed by Vitalik Buterin himself. Almost as sweet as the feeling when you buy an NFT below floor price.

Ingredients

  • Chocolate Ice cream
  • Milk
  • Banana
  • Ground Cinnamon

Steps

  1. Put 2 scoops chocolate ice cream in blender
  2. put banana and a cup of milk in blender
  3. Blend 1 minute
  4. Pour into cup and top with ground cinnamon
  5. Serve
