Description

A beautiful non-fungible burger with sweet caramalized onions, American cheese, all organic beef, on a brioche bun. Fabulous if paired with a side of delicious non-fungible fries and shake. Perfect to enjoy while you scroll through your mult-million dollar NFT collection.

Ingredients

  • Brioche bun
  • American cheese
  • Organic Beef patty
  • onions
  • Butter

Steps

  1. Butter the grill
  2. Begin grilling onions, beef patty, and buns
  3. Grill onions until caramalized, grill patty to customer's desired readiness, grill bun to lightly toasted
  4. Once burger is close to desired readiness, add american cheese to melt
  5. once cheese is melted. add patty to toasted buns and add caramalized onions above cheese
  6. Plate and serve
